About me

I'm a game developer who's passionate about programming and has almost four years of experience on the industry.

Video games have always piqued my interest, and I grew up playing a wide variety of them, including MMORPGs, shooter games, strategy games, and more. Initially, I was simply curious about how these games were made, but my interest soon turned into a deep fascination with building them.

Working on game development is an amazing and thoroughly enjoyable experience, and I feel incredibly fortunate to be able to do something I'm passionate about.

3D Turn-based Tactic Strategy Game

Meroth is a tactical strategy game, placed in a fantasy world and it's played by four players in a local device. This game was developed as a final project for my Digital Games graduation at UNIFACISA University.
